Is Credifyy regulated by the RBI?

Credifyy partners with RBI-registered NBFCs, ensuring a secure and compliant loan process in line with industry standards.

What is Credifyy and how does it work?

Credifyy is an app that offers instant personal loans and mutual fund investment services. You can apply for loans quickly and securely with paperless processing and flexible Credit Card options.

How can I apply for a loan through the Credifyy app?

Download the Credifyy app, register using your Aadhaar number, complete a simple 4-step process, and get your loan amount credited to your bank account in minutes.

What types of loans are available on Credifyy?

Credifyy offers personal loans ranging from INR 10,000 to INR 35,000, with instant approval and flexible Credit Card terms tailored to your needs.

What are the steps to apply for a personal loan with Credifyy?

Install the app, register via Aadhaar, complete your details, and once approved, receive your loan amount instantly into your bank account.

What are the eligibility requirements to get a loan on Credifyy?

To qualify, you must be an Indian salaried professional earning at least INR 25,000 per month, over 21 years old, and receive salary credits via direct bank transfers.

How soon will the loan amount be credited after approval?

Once approved, the loan amount is credited to your bank account in less than 15 minutes.

What is the process for repaying my loan on Credifyy?

Credifyy offers flexible Credit Card options with no pCredit Card or foreclosure charges, making it easy to manage your loan at your convenience.

How can I download and set up the Credifyy app?

You can download the Credifyy app from the app store, register using your Aadhaar number, and complete the verification process via OTP.

Is using the Credifyy app secure for personal and financial information?

Yes, Credifyy ensures the safety of your information through partnerships with RBI-registered NBFCs and a fully automated, secure loan processing system.
